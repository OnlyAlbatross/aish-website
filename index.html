The Story

Why AISH exists

"I was sick of choosing between my macros and actually feeling good."

I have had chronic digestion issues for most of my life. Every protein bar I tried left me bloated, sluggish, or worse. So I started making my own, drawing on Ayurvedic ingredients my family has used for generations — cardamom and ginger for digestion, whole food protein that your body actually recognizes.

After testing two drops across 30+ customers in Brooklyn and NYC, it was clear many people are suffering through the same thing. AISH is for anyone who is tired of processed, disgusting protein bars that treat performance and feeling good as mutually exclusive.

— Abhi, Founder & maker
